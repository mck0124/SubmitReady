Privacy policy

Privacy Policy for SubmitReady

Effective date: June 14, 2026

SubmitReady is designed as a privacy-first static web application. The core file tools run in your browser and do not require you to create an account or send your selected files to a SubmitReady server.

Files you choose

When you select images, PDFs, screenshots, or other files, the files are read by your browser so the selected tool can create a converted, optimized, cropped, masked, merged, split, rebuilt, renamed, or zipped output. The current site does not upload, store, sell, or share those selected files.

Local browser data

SubmitReady may store a small theme preference, such as light mode or dark mode, in your browser local storage. This preference is used only to keep the interface consistent when you return.

Third-party resources

SubmitReady may load open-source browser libraries from a third-party content delivery network, such as unpkg.com, to support PDF processing. These providers may receive standard request information such as your IP address, browser type, referring page, and request time. Selected local files are not intentionally sent to those providers by SubmitReady.

Advertising and analytics

This site may apply for advertising services such as Google AdSense in the future. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to SubmitReady or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Users can also visit aboutads.info to learn about opting out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising.

You can also review how Google uses data when you use partners' sites or apps .

Contact and removal requests

Because SubmitReady does not maintain user accounts or server-side file storage in the current version, there is no hosted user file database to delete. For privacy questions, correction requests, and advertising compliance inquiries, contact minchan0124@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as new features are added. Material changes should be reflected on this page with a new effective date.
